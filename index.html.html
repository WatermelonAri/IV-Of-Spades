First Article

posted on 23 March 2024, By Arianna.

One of the most famous bands in the philippines are the Iv of SpadesThey are a Filipino rock band from Metro Manila, Philippines, consisting of vocalist, bassist and keyboardist Zild Benitez, vocalist and lead guitarist Blaster Silonga, and drummer Badjao De castro. Unique served as the band's lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist until he left the band in 2018.

IV of spades description.Iv of Spades are known for having several musical awards both in Philippines and internationally, the band is also known for their 1970s-inspired outfit and their famous songs called "Mundo" and "Come inside Of My Heart".Their musical style has been described as "pop funk" and funk rock by the local press.

The band first gained exposure with the release of their 4th single "Mundo"on August 21, 2020the band entered an indefinite hiatus. Their sound is a blend of alternative rock and neo-soul infused with the traditional Filipino Folk Music. IV of spades formed in 2014 when their manager, Allan Silonga, was on a search for band members who could collaborate with his son Blaster. The band was formed in 2014/

Unique left Iv of Spades to pursue a solo career. When they disbanded the band members still continued to make music. Iv of Spades signed a record label and released their first single "Ilaw sa Daan" under Warner Music Philippines. The group's second single "Hey Barbara". was released on July 9,2017 and followed up by their third single "Where have you been, My disco?" released on December 9, 2017 via Youtube.
